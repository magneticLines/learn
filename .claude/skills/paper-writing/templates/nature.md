# Nature Family Journal Template

> Target: Nature, Nature Communications, Nature Methods, Scientific Reports, etc.

## Structure

```
Title (≤90 characters recommended)

Abstract (≤150 words, no references, no subheadings)

Main Text:
- Introduction (no heading in Nature-style)
- Results
- Discussion
- Methods (often after Discussion or in Supplementary)

Online Methods / Extended Data (if applicable)

References (numbered, max 50 for Letters; check per-journal limits)

Acknowledgements
Author Contributions
Competing Interests
```

## Key Style Rules
- **No subheadings in Introduction**
- Results and Discussion are **separate sections** (unlike IMRaD journals)
- Methods placed **after Discussion** (or online-only for Letters)
- Figures: max 6–8 in main text; use Extended Data for additional figures
- Reference format: numbered superscripts in text; full ref list at end
- Statistical reporting: always include n, error bars definition, and p-values

## Word Limits (check specific journal)
| Format | Words |
|--------|-------|
| Article | ~4000 (main text) |
| Letter | ~1500 |
| Review | ~8000 |

## Figure Guidelines
- Resolution: ≥300 dpi for photographs, ≥600 dpi for line art
- Format: PDF, EPS, or high-res TIFF
- Width: 89 mm (1 column) or 183 mm (2 columns)
