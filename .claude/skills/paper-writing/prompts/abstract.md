# Prompt — Abstract Generator

## Task
Generate a structured abstract from a full paper draft.

## Abstract Types
- **Structured** (Background / Methods / Results / Conclusions) — common in medicine and life sciences
- **Unstructured** — single paragraph, common in CS and engineering
- **中文摘要** — 目的、方法、结果、结论四要素

## Instructions

1. Read the full paper and identify: research problem, methodology, key results, and conclusions.
2. Write the abstract in the appropriate format based on the target journal.
3. Keep length within 150–300 words (English) or 200–400 字 (Chinese).
4. Do NOT introduce information not present in the paper.
5. Use past tense for methods and results; present tense for conclusions and implications.
6. End with a sentence on significance or future directions.

## Keywords
After the abstract, suggest 5–8 keywords that accurately represent the paper's content and are likely to be used in database searches.
