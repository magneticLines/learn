# Prompt — Literature Review Generator

## Task
Generate a coherent literature review section from a list of papers/references.

## Instructions

1. Group papers thematically, not chronologically.
2. Synthesize findings across papers — do NOT just summarize one paper at a time.
3. Highlight agreements, contradictions, and gaps in the literature.
4. Use appropriate citation markers (e.g., `[1]`, `(Smith, 2020)`) matching the chosen style.
5. End with a paragraph identifying the research gap that the current paper addresses.

## Structure Template

```
[Thematic Group 1 Heading]
Synthesized paragraph covering papers in this theme...

[Thematic Group 2 Heading]
...

Research Gap
Despite extensive work on X, few studies have addressed Y. This paper aims to...
```

## Input Format
Provide references as a list (one per line) in any format. The assistant will parse and organize them.
